About me

I have a wide range of interests that keep me engaged, curious, and always eager to learn. My passion for technology is a major driving force, fueling my creativity and drive to innovate. I’m particularly drawn to problem-solving, team leading, and programming—three areas where I feel I can make a tangible impact. I find programming especially fulfilling because it offers endless opportunities to build, troubleshoot, and bring ideas to life. Experimenting with new languages and frameworks is something I love; it keeps me agile and enables me to stay at the forefront of the tech landscape.

Beyond the world of tech, I am an avid fan of storytelling in all its forms. I enjoy immersing myself in anime, mystery and sci-fi novels, and comics, where I can explore diverse worlds filled with complex characters and creative plots. My taste in movies and series is equally varied, from classic films to modern releases—each story offering fresh perspectives and inspiration.

Professionally, my journey at RelianceCloud has been marked by diverse and progressively responsible roles, including DevOps Team Leader, Solutions Architect, and Junior AWS Technical Engineer. Each role has broadened my expertise and honed my practical skill set across AWS services, web development, project management, and automation. Leading the company’s first DevOps team was a pivotal moment, where I implemented CI/CD pipelines and automated processes that streamlined operations, bolstered efficiency, and improved system performance. In this capacity, I learned to balance technical rigor with adaptability and to champion continuous improvement in every project.

In my role as Solutions Architect, I collaborated closely with clients to design scalable, robust cloud solutions and oversaw the software development lifecycle, refining my skills in cloud architecture and automation. Concurrently, I actively engaged in software development, crafting applications and features that not only met client requirements but also enhanced user experiences. This dual focus allowed me to seamlessly integrate my development work with architectural oversight, ensuring that solutions were both functional and efficient.

My experience as Project Manager of Tech Support at IT Varsity provided me with further opportunities to enhance my project management, team coordination, and client relationship skills. Balancing the need to resolve technical issues promptly with maintaining high service standards was a challenge that helped me develop resilience and adaptability. During my time there, I also contributed to software development initiatives, collaborating with my team to create solutions that addressed various technical challenges and improved our service offerings.

Alongside my technical skills, my roles have strengthened my organizational abilities and taught me the importance of time management, clear communication, and a cohesive team dynamic. I am particularly skilled at analyzing business operations to identify improvement opportunities and implementing innovative solutions. My commitment to building a collaborative work environment allows me to foster productive relationships with colleagues and clients alike.

Recently, I’ve discovered a creative outlet in photography. Capturing real-life scenarios and fleeting moments that reveal unique details has become a new passion of mine. Photography lets me observe and appreciate the beauty in everyday life, whether it’s an expressive face, a meaningful interaction, or a perfectly framed landscape.

Altogether, my enthusiasm for technology, love for storytelling, and new interest in photography give me a balanced, well-rounded perspective. These passions keep me inspired and continually exploring new ways to understand and engage with the world around me. Looking forward, I am excited to bring my skills and experiences to a team that values innovation and collaboration and to contribute in a way that makes a meaningful impact.
